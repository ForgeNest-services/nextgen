Our Story

Founded in 2026 (2083 B.S.), NextGen Sports Nepal is committed to fueling the future of Nepal's athletes. We are passionate about discovering and developing young talent by creating opportunities for students to learn, grow, and excel through sports.

In association with the Nepal Swimming Association, UIPM Nepal, and the Nepal Fencing Association, we collaborate with schools and communities to provide quality training and athlete development programs. Our goal is to inspire the next generation with the values of discipline, teamwork, and excellence, while contributing to a stronger sporting culture in Nepal.

At NextGen Sports Nepal, we believe that every young athlete deserves the opportunity to reach their full potential and represent the future of Nepal with pride.

Mission & Values

Our core values guide every program, partnership, and decision we make.

Discipline

We instill commitment, focus, and the mental strength required to overcome challenges and achieve excellence through consistent effort.

Teamwork

We believe in the power of collaboration. Athletes grow stronger when they work together, support one another, and celebrate shared victories.

Excellence

We pursue the highest standards in coaching, training programs, and athlete development, never settling for mediocrity.

Our Vision

"To create an ecosystem where every young athlete in Nepal has access to professional sports training, mentorship, and competitive pathways—empowering them to achieve their dreams while representing Nepal on national and international stages."

Our Associations

We partner with leading national sports organizations to deliver world-class programs.

Nepal Swimming Association

Leading the development of swimming excellence across Nepal with certified coaches and international standards.

UIPM Nepal

Nepal Fencing Association
